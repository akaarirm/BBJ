URGENT CARE

Injuries happen fast, we treat you fast

When you know you need an orthopedic doctor but don't want to spend hours in the emergency room, come to us.  We are here with quality medical care.

Why Choose Our Urgent Care

  • Immediate access to a licensed, board certified, orthopedic health care provider
  • Avoid long waits at an overcrowded emergency room
  • Immediate access to modern diagnostic services
  • Much less expensive than a visit to the emergency room
  • 4 Convenient Locations:  Boulder, Louisville, Northglenn & Wes

WHAT WE DO


  • Sports Injuries
  • Sprains, Strains, Broken Bones & Fractures
  • Work Injuries
  • On-Site MRI & X-Ray

OUR SPECIALISTS


Our staff is dedicated to helping our patients obtain a prompt and expert diagnosis, as well as specialized treatment that is state-of-the-art. You will find the most advanced technology and a wide range of orthopedic expertise and services at Boulder Bone & Joint Urgent Care.
